Program Description

A tuition-based after school program is provided daily from 3:00 pm - 6:00 pm with several options available. In addition, numerous enrichment programs are offered each semester. Children do not have to be enrolled in the daycare portion of the program to participate in the enrichment classes. Enrollment for all programs is limited. The Eagle's Nest After School Program is managed by (CIASP).

Program Overview:

The school is a Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) Magnet school and offers 5 specialty classes, Science Lab, Digital Literacy, Math Lab, Musical Theater, and Physical Education. Students have the opportunity for in-depth, process-oriented studies of STEM disciplines in conjunction with a strong academic program. These studies develop critical-thinking and problem-solving through relevant and meaningful STEM explorations. Student centered learning emphasizes "hands-on" activities with real world applications.
